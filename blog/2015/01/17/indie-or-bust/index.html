The story of Cosmic Badger
17 Jan 2015

Indie or Bust

This month marks an important milestone. There is a complete build of a html5 game sitting on my phone and I’m currently gearing up to release on both iOS and Android. The journey up to this point has been no easy task, there have been a lot of hurdles to overcome and even though it seems that the hardest part is over I’m now facing an even more difficult exercise or raising awareness about the game so people will actually play it.

This blog is a retrospective history of the development of the game, from initial conception through to completion.
